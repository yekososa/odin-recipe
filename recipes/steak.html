steak

This is a recipe to create the perfect steak. I love to do a reverse sear. It gives you full control, extreme precision, and makes it almost impossible to screw up.

Ingredients

  • steak
  • salt
  • avocado oil

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 275 degrees F
  2. pat steak dry and salt generously
  3. place on wire rack in over until it reaches about 120 degrees internal
  4. sear that thing to your liking on a hot pan
